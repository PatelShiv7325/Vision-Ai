Capabilities
Everything you need

From enrollment to reporting — Vision AI handles the entire lifecycle of student attendance with precision.

LBPH Face Recognition
Local Binary Pattern Histogram algorithm provides robust recognition across different lighting conditions and angles.
Liveness Detection
Advanced anti-spoofing checks detect blinks, movement, and texture to prevent photo-based attendance fraud.
Bulk Group Capture
Capture an entire classroom in one photo. Recognizes multiple faces simultaneously for instant group marking.
Rich Analytics
Subject-wise breakdowns, attendance trends, and low-attendance alerts give faculty actionable insights.
Persistent Storage
All data stored in SQLite with face encodings, attendance logs, and student profiles preserved reliably.
Responsive Design
Fully mobile-optimized dashboards for both students and faculty. Works seamlessly on any device or screen size.

Process

in four steps
01
Register & Enroll
Student creates an account and captures their face via webcam during registration. Face encoding is stored securely.
02
Faculty Sets Up
Faculty registers their profile, adds subject details, and configures their attendance workflow.
03
Capture & Recognize
In class, faculty points a camera at students. Vision AI identifies all faces in real-time and marks attendance instantly.
04

Both students and faculty get detailed dashboards with subject-wise stats, trends, and downloadable reports.
